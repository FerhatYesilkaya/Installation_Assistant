Prerequisites
- Check in "configurables.ini" if the entry "MirthAdministratorSetupFileName" and "MirthConnectSetupFileName" have the correct value. This is the filename of the mirth connect and mirth Administrator Setups.

- Check in "configurables.ini" if the entry "intersystemsjdbcName" and "mirthConnectServiceName" have the correct value. This is the filename of the jdbc Drivers.
--> The Installation assistant is using the jdbc Driver in the "Intersystems Driver"-Folder not the "Intersystems Drivers"-Folder. There are two Folders with same files. 

How-to-use

1. Start "installation_assistant.exe"
2. Choose if you want to update or a new Installation
3. Fill in all the inputfields 
4. Press the "Start"-Button
